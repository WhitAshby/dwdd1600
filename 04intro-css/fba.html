Local North Shore Favorites

Food

Discover the best local eateries and food trucks that serve authentic Hawaiian cuisine.

Map of food truck locations

Top Favorites

  1. Giovanni's Shrimp Truck
  2. Matsumoto Shave Ice
  3. Seven Brothers
  • Foodland - A local grocery store with a great selection of Hawaiian snacks.
  • Leonard's Bakery - Famous for its malasadas (Portuguese doughnuts).
  • North Shore Tacos - Known for its fresh fish tacos.
  • Waialua Bakery - Offers a variety of baked goods and coffee.
  • Cholo's Homestyle Mexican - A local favorite for Mexican food.
  • Huli Huli Chicken - A popular spot for grilled chicken.
  • Shark's Cove Grill - Great for casual dining with a view.
  • Sunset Beach Grill - Offers burgers and local dishes with a beach view.
  • Waimea Bay Grill - Known for its breakfast and lunch options.
  • Seven Brothers - A food truck offering a variety of burgers.

Beaches

Explore the stunning beaches known for their beauty and surf culture.

Famous North Shore Beaches

Top Beaches

  1. Waimea Bay
  2. Sunset Beach
  • Waimea Bay - Perfect for swimming in summer and surfing in winter.
  • Sunset Beach - Known for its breathtaking sunsets and big waves.
  • Banzai Pipeline - Famous for its powerful waves, ideal for experienced surfers.
  • Shark's Cove - A great spot for snorkeling and tide pooling.
  • Haleiwa Beach Park - Family-friendly with picnic areas and calm waters.

Activities

Engage in various activities that highlight the natural beauty of the North Shore.

What to do on the North Shore

Top Activities

  1. Surfing Lessons
  2. Turtle Watching
  • Snorkeling at Shark's Cove - A great spot to see marine life.
  • Hiking to Waimea Falls - Enjoy a scenic hike with a waterfall reward.
  • Turtle Watching at Laniakea Beach - Spot sea turtles basking on the shore.
